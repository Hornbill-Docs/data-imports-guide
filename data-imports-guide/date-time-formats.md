# Date & Time Format Options

Below is the list of Date, Time and DateTime Formats that are recognized by Hornbill: 

## Date Formats

| Format | Example|
|:--|:--|
| dd MM yyyy | 21 02 2020|
| dd-MM-yyyy | 21-02-2020|
| dd/MM/yyyy | 21/02/2020|

| MMM dd yyyy | Feb 21 2020|
| MMM-dd-yyyy | Feb-21-2020|
| MMM-dd-yy | Feb-21-20|
| MMM dd yy | Feb 21 20|

## Time Formats

| Format | Example|
|:--|:--|
| HH:mm | 23:30|
| HH:mm:ss | 23:30:25|
| h:mm tt | 11:30 PM|
| h:mm:ss tt | 11:30:25 PM|
